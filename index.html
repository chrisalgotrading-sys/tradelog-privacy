Privacy Policy

Chrome Extension
TradeLog does not collect, transmit, or store any user data on external servers. All your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

TradeLog does not collect any personal information. The extension does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying information.

All trade records, account transactions, capital entries, and settings are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage using the chrome.storage.local API. This data lives on your own device and is never transmitted anywhere.

The developer has no access to your trading data at any time.

External Network Requests

TradeLog makes one type of external network request: a public GET request to api.frankfurter.app to retrieve currency exchange rates published by the European Central Bank.

This request:

  • Contains no user data
  • Contains no identifiers
  • Contains no personal information of any kind
  • Is made at most once per hour
  • Results are cached locally on your device

The sole purpose of this request is to calculate accurate pip values for forex instruments.

Data Sharing

No user data is sold, transferred, or shared with any third party, under any circumstances.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Clear all data" button in the Extension settings tab. Uninstalling the extension also removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

TradeLog does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13. The extension is intended for adult traders.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the extension's support page in the Chrome Web Store.
