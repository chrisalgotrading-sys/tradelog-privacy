Privacy Policy

Chrome Extension
TradeLog stores all your trading data locally on your device using chrome.storage.local. Your trade data is never transmitted to any external server.

Data Collection

TradeLog collects the following data:

  • Trade records (instrument, direction, prices, P&L, dates, notes, setup, confidence) — stored locally on your device only
  • Capital transactions (deposits and withdrawals) — stored locally on your device only
  • Setup configurations and application settings — stored locally on your device only
  • Email address — collected only when purchasing a Pro subscription, used solely for subscription verification and restoration
  • Pro subscription token (JWT) — stored locally on your device to verify Pro access

Data Storage

All trade data, transactions, setups, settings, and your Pro token are stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) on your own device. This data never leaves your device.

Your email address and subscription token are stored on our backend server (hosted on Netlify, United States) solely to allow subscription verification and restoration if you reinstall the extension.

Data Handling and Use

  • Trade data — used locally to compute statistics, charts, and analytics. Never transmitted externally.
  • Email address — used solely to verify and restore your Pro subscription. Never used for marketing or shared with third parties.
  • Pro token — used locally to authenticate your Pro access on each launch.

External Network Requests

TradeLog makes limited external requests for specific purposes only:

  • api.frankfurter.app — retrieves live currency exchange rates. No personal data is sent. Results are cached locally.
  • tradelog-backend.netlify.app — verifies Pro subscription status using your email address. Only contacted when activating or restoring a Pro subscription.
  • stripe.com — processes subscription payments. Governed by Stripe's own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment card details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose. The only third parties that may process your data are Stripe (payments) and Netlify (hosting), as described above.

Data Retention and Deletion

All local data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time via the "Clear all data" button in the History tab. Uninstalling the extension also removes all locally stored data.

To request deletion of your email address from our backend, contact us at achrisalgotrading@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

TradeLog is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new effective date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
